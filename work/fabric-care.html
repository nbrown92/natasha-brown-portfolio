Procter & Gamble · Fabric Enhancers · Product Researcher
In Development

Olfactory ritual over functional performance — a $25M sub-brand launch.

A 12-week longitudinal in-home study tracking product adoption across three consumer segments — uncovering that adoption was driven by olfactory ritual rather than functional performance. That behavioral insight directly informed the positioning and naming of a new sub-brand that generated $25M in first-year sales, and connected consumer behavior to R&D formulation decisions.
